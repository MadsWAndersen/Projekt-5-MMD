    Icons need to be used the right way. The icons in this product have therefore been used because there is meaning behind them. They add to the overall style of the products and keep the pages from turning into nothing but boxes with text. Icons help make the pages more dynamic.

    LANGUAGE/TONE OF VOICE

    Formality

    The language needs to be somewhat formal though also easy to understand so that the user understands the product and how to use it, while the product as well as the product owner still comes across as reliable and secure. However, the product should also be able to speak to a younger target group which is why it can't be too formal.
